Mission and Vision

Omama is committed to comfort, care and service

BACKGROUND

COMFORT.HOME.FAMILY
As age progresses, there are certain daily activities that become extremely challenging for elderly loved folks.

In this regard, it is impossible to take very good care of themselves without any assistance. This is where Omama Home health comes in.

At omama home health, we provide a wide range of services specifically tailored for those who needs them most. Choosing omama means opting for convenient, reliable and compassionate care for loved ones.

OUR MISSION

To help clients to maintain their autonomy and quality of life in their homes and provide appropriate trained and skilled staff that fit our clients’ needs to ensure cohesion of care wherever possible.

OUR VISION

The overriding principle surrounding our vision is that, we aim to be a first class and outstanding care provider, providing our clients with state of the arts care using every possible legitimate means available to achieve the best possible outcomes for them.
